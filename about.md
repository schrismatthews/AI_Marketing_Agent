# About jaredrhod

## Who Jared is
Jared Rhodenizer (@jaredrhod) is an operator who runs several real businesses on an AI system he and his AI agent built together. He started posting about that workflow on social media, it went viral, and jaredrhod is the brand that came out of it: teaching the practical, money-making side of AI to entrepreneurs, operators, and the next generation. He is not a prompt guru. The edge is that everything he teaches, he actually runs.

## What jaredrhod teaches
The cool half of AI is everywhere. The half that pays is not. Jared teaches the half that pays. Five pillars:
1. External memory: giving your AI a real, persistent memory that lives outside the model (the AI Memory Vault).
2. Giving the AI context and having the AI load that context from the Obsidian vault before doing any work.
3. The structure that aims it: organizing that memory so the AI pulls back exactly what it needs in one step.
4. An AI workforce: standing jobs and agents that do real work for you.
5. The marketing fundamentals that make all of it actually make money.
